DECIPHER MY THOUGHTS
1.
You still ask me about the day of my birth
So write down what you don't know
The day you declared your love
Is the day of my birth.
2.
My lover asks me:
"What is the difference between me and the sky?"
The difference, my love,
Is that when you laugh,
I forget about the sky.
3.
Because my love for you
Is higher than words,
I have decided to fall silent.
POETRY BY NIZAR KABBANI FROM "ARABIAN LOVE POEMS"
